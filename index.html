The World's Most Versatile Printer

About The Omniprint

The goal of the Omniprint is to solve all of the problems in 3D printing in one machine. The Omniprint can switch between heads, use different filaments in one print, smooth finishes, and more!

Features of the Omniprint

Head Switching

The Omniprint can quickly and automatically switch between heads, so you don't have to balance speed and detail.
Filament Switching

The Omniprint's ability to swap heads can be used to easily transition between materials. For example, you could print all of your supports in a water-soluable material for easy removal.
Subtractive Elements

The Omniprint can use specially designed subtractive elements, such as a sander, router, or drill bit in order to do even more things that most printers cannot.
